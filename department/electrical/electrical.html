Message from Head of Department

Electrical Engineering is the backbone of modern infrastructure and smart industry. Our department is committed to building technically strong, responsible, and industry-ready diploma engineers.

We focus on hands-on learning in power systems, electrical machines, control engineering, and measurement techniques through well-equipped laboratories and practical sessions.

Along with academics, we guide students in mini-projects, teamwork, communication skills, and professional ethics so they can confidently perform in industry as well as higher education.

Our goal is to create skilled and disciplined electrical engineers who can contribute effectively to energy, automation, maintenance, and sustainable development sectors.

Mr. Bichkule P.B

Head of Department - Electrical Engineering
