Message from Head of Department

Our department is committed to delivering quality technical education.

The Department of Computer Engineering is one of the leading and progressive departments of the institute. The department provides quality education in modern technologies such as programming, web development, database management, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

With experienced and dedicated faculty members, well-equipped computer laboratories, and practical-based teaching methods, the department ensures the overall development of students. Various technical workshops, seminars, project competitions, and industrial visits are regularly organized to connect students with the industry.

Our objective is not only to provide academic knowledge but also to develop technical skills, innovative thinking, research attitude, and ethical values among students. The department aims to produce competent and industry-ready computer engineers who can contribute effectively to society and technological advancement.

Prof. Arati S. Thorat

Head of Department - Computer Engineering
