About Us

Suhas Institute of Technology, Kasurdi is approved by AICTE & DTE Maharashtra and affiliated to MSBTE, offering quality diploma engineering education with a student-centric and practice-oriented approach.

We focus on overall student development, including academics, practical skills, discipline, teamwork, and leadership. Our modern labs, skilled faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum prepare students for higher education, industry, and entrepreneurship.

Located in a safe and eco-friendly campus at Kasurdi, Tal. Daund, Dist. Pune, we provide a supportive and motivating learning environment.

Vision

To ensure sustainable human development, encourage a self-reliant and self-content society, and promote community service and social welfare through quality education at affordable cost.

Mission

To impart state-of-the-art education and technical expertise to students, provide necessary training to teachers, and build a responsible, self-reliant society through high-quality academic programs and ethical participation in public life.
