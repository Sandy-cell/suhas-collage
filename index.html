About Us

Suhas Institute of Technology, Karsurdi is approved by AICTE & DTE Maharashtra and affiliated to MSBTE, offering quality diploma engineering education with a student-centric and practice-oriented approach.

We focus on overall student development, including academics, practical skills, discipline, teamwork, and leadership. Our modern labs, skilled faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum prepare students for higher education, industry, and entrepreneurship.

Located in a safe and eco-friendly campus at Karsurdi, Tal. Daund, Dist. Pune, we provide a supportive and motivating learning environment.
